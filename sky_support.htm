Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I become a tester?

Simply register here, create a profile, and start browsing available test jobs. Read our guidelines to understand expectations and maximize your earnings.

How do I create a test job as a developer?

Sign up as a developer, go to your dashboard, and follow the step-by-step guide to create a test job. You can specify test requirements and monitor feedback in real-time.

What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

Check our troubleshooting guide below, or reach out to support using the contact form at the bottom of this page.

Technical Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, try the following steps:

  • Make sure you’re using a supported browser version.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Refer to our technical documentation for compatibility requirements.
