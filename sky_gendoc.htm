Introduction

Welcome to Skylab! This document provides general guidelines for all users of the platform, including developers, testers, and job providers.

Roles Overview

Skylab is a platform where multiple roles interact to achieve quality results. Here's a quick overview:

  • Developers: Use Skylab to create testing jobs and gather feedback on their projects.
  • Testers: Perform testing tasks and provide actionable insights.
  • Job Providers: Manage tasks and ensure timely payments for testers.

General Guidelines

  • Maintain clear communication with other users of the platform.
  • Ensure all submissions (code, tests, feedback) adhere to platform quality standards.
  • Respect deadlines to keep the platform efficient and reliable.
  • Provide accurate details when creating jobs or submitting feedback.
  • Keep your account details and payment information up to date.

Platform Rules

  1. No Plagiarism: All content submitted must be original.
  2. Respect Others: Be courteous in communications.
  3. Timely Payments: Job providers must release payments promptly upon task completion.
  4. Transparency: Provide honest and constructive feedback.
  5. Adherence to Policies: Follow Skylab’s terms and conditions at all times.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Who can use Skylab?

Anyone with skills in software development, testing, or project management can join Skylab.

How do I resolve conflicts?

Use the platform's built-in dispute resolution system or contact Skylab support.

Can I switch roles?

Yes, users can register for multiple roles and switch as needed.
