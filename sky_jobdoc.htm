Introduction

Welcome to Skylab's Job Provider Documentation. This guide explains how to create testing tasks, manage submissions, and ensure testers deliver optimal results.

Creating Jobs

To create a new job, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Skylab account and navigate to the "Job Management" section.
  2. Click on Create New Job.
  3. Fill in the details:
    • Job Title: A concise name for the task.
    • Description: Detailed steps for testers.
    • Testing Type: Functional, Performance, UI/UX, etc.
    • Devices: Specify target devices (e.g., mobile, desktop).
    • Deadline: Set an appropriate completion time.
    • Budget: Allocate funds for the task.
  4. Review and submit the job for approval.

Managing Jobs

Once your job is live, manage it as follows:

  • Monitor submissions on the Job Dashboard.
  • Review feedback and issue reports submitted by testers.
  • Approve or request revisions for submitted reports.
  • Communicate with testers if clarification is needed.
  • Close the job once all tasks are completed.

Payment Guidelines

Payments ensure testers are rewarded fairly. Here's how to manage payments:

  1. Set the budget while creating a job. You can specify:
    • Per Tester: Fixed amount per tester.
    • Overall Budget: Distributed among all testers.
  2. Funds will be held in escrow until job completion.
  3. Release payments after approving reports.

Note: Skylab charges a 10% platform fee on all transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What types of jobs can I post?

You can post jobs for functional testing, UI/UX feedback, performance testing, and more.

How do I ensure quality feedback?

Provide detailed instructions and examples. Use Skylab’s feedback template for testers.

Can I set tester requirements?

Yes, you can filter testers by skills, location, or device capabilities when creating a job.
