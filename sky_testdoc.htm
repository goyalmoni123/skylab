Introduction

Welcome to Skylab's Tester Documentation. This guide helps you understand how to participate in testing, log issues, and provide valuable feedback.

Getting Started

  1. Sign up as a tester on the Skylab platform.
  2. Complete your profile with relevant testing skills and devices.
  3. Browse available test jobs on your dashboard.
  4. Accept tasks that match your expertise and interest.

Testing Tasks

When assigned a testing task, follow these steps:

  • Download or access the product as instructed.
  • Use the provided checklist to perform the required tests.
  • Document bugs or issues clearly with screenshots or recordings.
  • Submit your test report through the platform.

Reporting Issues

When reporting issues, ensure you include the following:

  • Steps to Reproduce: Describe how the issue occurs.
  • Environment: Device, browser, OS, and version.
  • Severity: Label the issue as Critical, Major, or Minor.
  • Attachments: Include screenshots, screen recordings, or logs.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I get paid for my tests?

Payments are processed once the developer approves your submitted reports. You can track payment status on your profile.

What types of testing are available?

We offer functional, UI/UX, performance, and compatibility testing tasks.

Can I collaborate with other testers?

Yes, some tasks allow team-based testing. You’ll find collaboration instructions in the task description.
