About Skylab

Empowering Developers and Testers through Cloud Collaboration

Our Mission

Skylab is a cloud-based platform dedicated to streamlining the development process for developers and testers alike. With our crowdsourced testing framework, we aim to revolutionize the way teams gather feedback, analyze performance, and ensure product quality. Whether you’re launching a new feature or scaling an existing application, Skylab provides the tools to help you succeed in today’s fast-paced digital environment.

Community Collaboration

Engage with a diverse testing community to get real-world feedback on your applications, enabling broader test coverage.

Automated Testing

Utilize automated testing with tools like Selenium, saving time and ensuring consistent, reliable results.

Performance Insights

Gain valuable insights from detailed analytics on your test jobs, helping you refine and optimize your applications.

Cloud Integration

Seamlessly integrate with cloud services to harness the power of remote testing and collaboration on a global scale.
